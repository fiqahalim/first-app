## Description
This is the sample application for the FoodBank.

### Overview of Application
1. Types of User
   - Food Donor or;
   - Food Consumer
2. Location for Foodbank; Listing of available food bank
   - List of available food bank in Malaysia
3. Types of Services
   - Delivery or;
   - Self Collect
4. Food Donor
   - Records of their requested
   - Details of their account
5. Food Consumer
   - Records of their submitted items
   - Details of their account
   - Show status of their account type either individual or business
